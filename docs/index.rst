**A LinkedIn-like website aiming to connect people by mutual companies, and universities.**

**There are 3 types of users: personal, company, and university. Personal profiles will be able to “connect” with each other and “follow” related companies and universities. All types of users can share posts but have different profile layouts.**

**We are using bootstrap, flask, and mysql technologies.**
